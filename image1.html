Max Temperature

As you can see from the graph the temperature is greatest in the souther hemisphere and increases as you get closer to the equator. As you go further toward the northern hemisphere the temperature begins to gradually decline. This is to be expected since we know that locations in the south tend to have higher tempertaures in the summer than northern locations. As well as less sevre winter.
